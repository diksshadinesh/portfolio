The CW: Network Rebrand

scroll
ABOUT

The CW is a TV Network that caters to both men and women from 10-34 years old, with original content most popularly The Flash, Supergirl and Arrow. The Network is also known for it's Emmy winning actresses Rachel Bloom and Gina Rodriguez. The current visual brand does not shine light on the variety or quality of content on the Network and falls short of creating a personality for the network and its many loyal fans. A network identity or station ID is a creatively branded broadcast animations that plays throughout the network to reinforce the brand’s identity. A network ID menu is an animation that informs the viewer about programing while reinforcing the visual brand of the network.

Concept and moodboard

The CW ‘Everyone's Invited’, is an effort to highlight the variety of shows on the network. The network caters to both men and women from the 18-34 demographic featuring shows with super heroes, zombies, light hearted dramas, musical comedies and improvisations. The use of multiple colors and icons represent the mood of each genre along with white typography to visually communicate the variety of shows on the network and the spirit of 'Everyone's Invited'.
