Research and Inspiration

The main source of inspiration for this piece was from "Smile, You’re Speaking EMOJI", an article by Adam Sternberg for New York Managize. The article talks about how emoji is now the world's fastest growing language and gets into the lingustics of this "wordless language.

"The 3,000-year-old tilde (~) might want to consider rebranding itself as Invisible Man With Twirled Mustache" - Adam Sternberg

Concept and Moodboard

“We’ve learned to talk, and we’ve learned to write, but we’re only now learning to write at the speed of talking.” -Adam Sternbergh. The psychology of emoji represents a much larger cultural shift in communication. To show the cultural weight they carry this infographic employs blod typography and icons to communicate the linguistics of emoji.

Animating on a grid

After listening to the song and editing it down to 30 seconds I listened to the beat and built a grid. Each box is 15 frames long, and then I planned different compositions around those blocks where the length of each composition is a multiple of 15 frames
